<p><b>Igor Lewczuk</b> (born 30 May 1985) is a Polish <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a href="/wiki/Legia_Warsaw" title="Legia Warsaw">Legia Warsaw</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Igor_Lewczuk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Club">Club</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Igor_Lewczuk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In January 2011, he was loaned to <a href="/wiki/Piast_Gliwice" title="Piast Gliwice">Piast Gliwice</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In June 2011, he was loaned to <a href="/wiki/Ruch_Chorz%C3%B3w" title="Ruch Chorzów">Ruch Chorzów</a> on a one-year deal.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 19 June 2014, Lewczuk signed three-year deal with <a href="/wiki/Legia_Warsaw" title="Legia Warsaw">Legia Warsaw</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 31 August 2016, French club <a href="/wiki/FC_Girondins_de_Bordeaux" title="FC Girondins de Bordeaux">Bordeaux</a> announced that Lewczuk would play for them for the next two seasons.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 12 June 2019, Lewczuk returned to <a href="/wiki/Legia_Warsaw" title="Legia Warsaw">Legia Warsaw</a> on a 1-year contract with an option for a further year.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
